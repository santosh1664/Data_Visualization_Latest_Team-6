Our Objective
The main goal of this project is to create a system that converts datasets to other non-visual formats and, eventually, to give speech to blind listeners. This system should: Convert Data to Tactile Graphics: Create tactile graphs representing data points, trends, and relationships for blind users to explore and understand the information through touch. Generate Textual Descriptions: To provide a complete picture, describe the data, context, and any patterns present. Translate Data to Auditory Representations: Transform data into auditory representations, where each sonic pattern or structure carries information, allowing the listener to experience data through sound. Develop Interactive Tools: Build intuitive interfaces for blind individuals to navigate the data, select individual points to interact with, and explore various dataset dimensions. Are you ready to unlock the potential of your data? Whether you have a specific data in mind or need guidance on how data visualization can benefit, we're here to help. Get in touch with us today, and let's start visualizing your data journey together.
Significance:
Solving this problem is essential for the inclusion of visually impaired people in data-rich information. By making data visualization and tools accessible, this project aims for more meaningful decision-making, engagement with data-driven content, and full inclusion in academic and professional domains. This will be a step towards ensuring the inclusion of blind people in an emerging data-driven world.
